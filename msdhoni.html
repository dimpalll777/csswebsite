🦁 MS Dhoni - Captain Cool 🦁

Introduction

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni or Captain Cool, is one of the greatest captains and wicketkeeper-batsmen in cricket history. He is famous for his calm nature, finishing ability, leadership skills, and helicopter shot. Dhoni has led Chennai Super Kings (CSK) to multiple IPL championships and inspired millions of cricket fans across the world.
